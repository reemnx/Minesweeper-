How to play:

To win this game, you have to expose ALL the none Mines Cells AND mark with a flag (Right Click) all the mines Cells.

Smiley Button: will restart the game after lose or win.

Hint Button: after Click on Hint, you have to chose any cell and expose it & neighbors for a second.

Safe Click Button: after Click, a random Non mine cell will be marked for a second.

Set Manually Bomb Button: after Click, chose any cell you want on the board to apply a bomb on it.
